Workshop on Cryptography and Information Security 2005

Tokyo, Japan, October 21, 2005


Announcement

The Workshop on Cryptography and Information Security is devoted to dissemination of knowledge in different aspects of information security. The invited talks will provide a tutorial in the selected areas of cryptography. Recent achievements, development trends and open problems will also be presented.


Venue

The workshop will be held on the Komaba Research Campus, Institute of Industial Science, the University of Tokyo.
All the talks will take place at the Komaba Convention Hall (Block An, Room An-201, 2nd floor).
