Accomodation for Invited Speakers


We recommend the invited speakers to stay at the Shinjuku Washington Hotel Annex.
The fee for two nights (approx. 27000 JPY) should be payed when checking-in.
You can choose to have breakfast (the standard "European" style) at the hotel (this will cost extra 1300 JPY per meal).


Local Attractions


Tokyo is Japan's capital and the country's largest city. It is also the country's largest center of politics, business, education, mass media, and pop culture. Tokyo has Japan's highest concentration of corporate headquarters, financial institutions, universities and colleges, museums, theaters, and shopping and entertainment establishments.

More information and facts can be found on the official and the unofficial (but very good) city guides.
